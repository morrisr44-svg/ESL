👗 Clothes

A1 Vocabulary Activity  |  Three Levels

Level 1 – Match the Clothes 🧩
Drag each picture to the correct word. Can you match them all?
Level 2 – Flip the Cards 🃏
Look at each picture. Write your answer, then press Check to see if you're right! 🎯
Use: It is a ______ It's a ______ This is a ______ These are ______ They are ______ They're ______
Level 3 – True or False? 🕵️
Choose a person. Read each sentence. Is it TRUE ✅ or FALSE ❌?
